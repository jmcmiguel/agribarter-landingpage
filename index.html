Get To Know Us.

E-Barter: Online Bartering System for Agricultural Products and Supplies is a web application focused on the process of exchanging agricultural goods and supplies. The system can be a great solution to avoid wasting excess products, and have available resources for the people who lack agricultural supplies and needs.

We developed a web application that can enhance the process of exchanging agricultural goods and supplies through the systemâ€™s usability, reliability, and efficiency. It can help small to medium-scale farmers and agricultural entrepreneurs promote their products and supplies and innovate their means of finding potential consumers through online bartering.

Our Objectives

The main objective is to develop an E-Barter: Online Bartering System for Agricultural Products and Supplies that is capable of providing traders in the agricultural sector an efficient way of trading agricultural products and supplies.
